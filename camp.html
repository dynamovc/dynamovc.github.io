About Founders Camp

Do you have a fire started? Let us fan the flames and help you take it to the next level.

In tribute to our hometown Chattanooga, TN, the capital for logistics technology and an epicenter for outdoor culture, we're proud to run a four-day "camp" to help founders build their business and drive meaningful traction through introductions to a variety of corporations. We have gathered a group of seasoned startup operators and forward-thinking corporations who can help move the needle for your business.

We are focused on founders building revolutionary businesses in supply chain and transportation, globally. Competitive companies will have a strong team, a product/service built, and early traction with customers/users. Those companies that are looking to raise a seed or Series A would benefit from our focus on relevant operational advice and customer introductions. There is no cost, cash or equity to participate. We just ask for a co-founder or equivalent to commit themselves from the evening of October 1st through lunch on October 5th.

Applications are open till August 1, 2018. Apply here. We expect to let participants know by August 30th about their status. We will invite up to up to 20 companies to participate. As part of your involvement, you will also recieve the benefits of the F6S Alphacard.

If you have any questions, please email us at hello@dynamo.vc and we will respond promptly.
